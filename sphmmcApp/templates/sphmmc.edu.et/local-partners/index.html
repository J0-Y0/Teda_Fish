Local partners

As we continue to broaden and strengthen our services with the goal of becoming a Center of Excellence, we have forged invaluable partnerships, both at home and abroad. Within Ethiopia, our collaborations serve to raise not only our own standards of care but also those of our colleagues in every section of the country.
