Training and consultancy

SPHMMC Library would provide short term capacity building trainings and consultancy service in the following areas:

  • Information Literacy.

  • Advanced Literature Searching on Research4Life/HINARI, PubMed, Scopes, etc.

  • Evidence Based Health Care Information Resources, Systematic Reviews.

  • Citation databases, Reference Management Software & Collaboration Tools.

  • Copyright, Licensing & Ethical Considerations.

  • Research Methodology.

  • Predatory Journals.

  • Library Management Consultancy.

  • KOHA ILS, Greenstone digital library software & DSpace.

  • Basic Library Services.

  • Basic Computer Training .
