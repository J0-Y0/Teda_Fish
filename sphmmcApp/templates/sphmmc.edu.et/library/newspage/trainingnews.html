Staff Capacity Building Training

  Alemayehu Bisrat       6:49 AM       Library News

St. Paul’s Library & Documentation Service Directorate had conducted an in-service training for new and old library staffs. The objective of the training was to impart trainees with necessary library skills which could help them provide effective and efficient day to day patron service.

The topics covered in the training includes Basic Library & Information Services, COVID-19 Mitigation in Library Environment, Introduction to Computer & the Internet, Information Literacy, KOHA Integrated LMS, Greenstone & Dspace Digital Library Software Systems.

The training took place at the library’s training room from 25th August to 10 September 2020 in three rounds and fully supported by the Academic & Research Vice Provost Office.
